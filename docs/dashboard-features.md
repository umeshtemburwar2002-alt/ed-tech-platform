# Dashboard Features

## Student Dashboard Features

* **Enrolled Courses** – View and access all active courses with quick resume and "last activity" tracking.
* **Progress Tracking** – Track lessons completed, test scores, time spent, and learning milestones with visual progress charts.
* **Course Recommendations** – AI-powered suggestions based on learning history, career goals, and trending industry skills.
* **Notifications / Announcements** – Instant updates about new courses, instructor messages, events, deadlines, and platform news.
* **Upcoming Tests & Assignments** – Calendar view with color-coded due dates, reminders, and direct submission links.
* **Company Assignments** – Access real-world project tasks from partnered companies with submission and review tracking.
* **Mock Interviews** – Scheduled practice interviews with performance scoring, detailed feedback, and reattempt opportunities.
* **Progress Report** – Downloadable/shareable PDF and interactive dashboard analytics showing strengths, weaknesses, and growth trends.
* **Badges & Certificates** – Earn, store, and showcase verified skill-based achievements on your profile and LinkedIn.
* **Doubt Forum / Live Q&A** – Threaded discussion boards, AI-assist answers, and real-time expert-led sessions.
* **Skill Gap Analysis** – Personalized reports highlighting improvement areas with recommended micro-courses.
* **Career Roadmap** – Step-by-step learning path with role-specific milestones, certifications, and mentorship guidance.
* **Leaderboard & Peer Competition** – See rankings based on performance, consistency, and skill mastery.
* **Resource Library** – Central hub for e-books, coding resources, and practice exercises.
* **Performance Alerts** – Early warning system for falling behind in lessons or assessments.
* **Internship & Job Board** – Curated industry opportunities matched to completed skills.

## Instructor Dashboard Features

* **My Courses** – Manage, organize, and monitor created courses with quick edit and duplication features.
* **Add / Edit Courses** – Upload video, documents, quizzes, and interactive assignments with built-in plagiarism checks.
* **View Student Progress** – Access individual and batch performance summaries with filtering options.
* **Analytics** – Track course completions, enrollments, ratings, watch time, and student engagement heatmaps.
* **Manage Tests & Assignments** – Create, schedule, auto-grade quizzes, and provide manual grading with feedback.
* **Company Project Integration** – Assign, track, and evaluate live industry projects with submission status.
* **Mock Interview Conduction** – Schedule, host, and assess mock interviews with recorded session playback.
* **Progress Report Generation** – Automated, customizable reports for individual students or groups.
* **Live Class Scheduling** – Host interactive sessions with polls, whiteboards, breakout rooms, and auto-recording.
* **Student Feedback & Ratings** – View feedback analytics, sentiment breakdown, and improvement suggestions.
* **Revenue & Earnings Tracking** – Monitor income, payout schedules, conversion rates, and sales funnels.
* **Collaboration Tools** – Co-create courses with other instructors, share teaching resources, and split revenues.
* **Assignment & Test Analytics** – See difficulty ratings, average scores, and question-level performance.
* **Marketing Tools** – Email campaigns, coupon creation, and social media sharing for course promotion.
* **AI Content Suggestions** – Get automated recommendations for new topics or course improvements.
* **Community Engagement** – Host instructor blogs, AMA (Ask Me Anything) sessions, and discussion groups.
